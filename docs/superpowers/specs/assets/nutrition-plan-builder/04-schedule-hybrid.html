How it maps: "Every day" edits write the same meal to all 7 schedule_entries for that slot (PUT /schedule/:day ×7). Switching to "Customize days" + editing one day writes only that day's entries (amber override). The week grid is a pure read of schedule_entries; a cell tap drops you into that day's override editor. Daily total bar = Σ meal macros vs the plan target (preview of the macro-totals decision, next).
