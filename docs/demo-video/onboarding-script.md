# CoachEasy Onboarding Demo Script

## Beat 1 — Start With The Coach's Business

This is CoachEasy. I’m starting as a coach with a blank business, and the first thing it does is show me the four paths that matter: finish my profile, invite a client, build coaching content, and publish a page for prospects.

It is not forcing me through a wizard. It is showing me the shape of the business I am setting up.

## Beat 2 — Build The Coaching Content

Next is the Library. This is where the coach builds the reusable parts of the business: exercises, foods, recipes, training plans, nutrition plans, and check-ins.

The coach authors the system here. Later, the client will follow that system in their own app.

## Beat 3 — Build A Training Plan

Here’s a training plan. I can build the structure of the week, add workouts, choose exercises, and prescribe the sets the client should follow.

This is the coach’s plan, not the client’s log. The plan says what should happen. Later, the client app records what actually happened.

## Beat 4 — Invite A Client And Assign The Plan

Now that the training plan exists, I can bring in a client and assign it. CoachEasy sends the email invite, tracks the pending acceptance, and creates the client record where the coach can work.

That client record becomes the hub for this relationship: plans, notes, check-ins, progress, workout history, and messages all live from here.
